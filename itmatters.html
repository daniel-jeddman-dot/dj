It matters

Show me your way, that I may walk with you. The cry of my life is to love you more. To live a life that is pleasing and pleasant to life. Give me a second chance to prove you right. I know you really understand all I am trying to say by now. I've counted on trees and waters. They never gave me any answers. My heart desired for the size of time. But time and life proved me wrong. There comes a stranger, from the highlands of heaven, his eyes like flames of fire. Why has though departed from thy glory given to you with no pain. I may be a stranger but my words are mightier. Believe in me and believe in my words.
